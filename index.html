ℹ️ DISCLAIMER
📋
This app is NOT a financial tool and does NOT promote or endorse any lending or financial activities.

BUBU System is designed solely for tracking and recording the savings contributions of group members (commonly known as "Bubu" in Filipino community savings groups).

Interest is calculated using simple interest: each 30-day period adds the same fixed amount (principal × rate). No compounding.

All data is stored locally on this device and is not transmitted anywhere.

Use responsibly. 🙏
